Senior Thesis

During my senior year of undergrad at Haverford College, I wrote my thesis on Algorithm Bias in the justice system.

Taking results from ProPublica's Machine Bias article, I extended it to show results for gender and gender and race in the COMPAS algorithm.

Using the dataset that ProPublica used, I wrote a Python script to analyze based on these contraints.
